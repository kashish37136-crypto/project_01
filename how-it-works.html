How It Works

Understanding the technology behind offline communication.

1. Mesh Networking

Unlike traditional networks that rely on a central server, a mesh network connects devices directly to one another. Each phone acts as a node.

2. Bluetooth LE

We use Bluetooth Low Energy to discover nearby devices without draining battery. It has a range of up to 100 meters.

3. Multi-hop Transfer

If you want to message someone far away, your message "hops" through other users' phones until it reaches the recipient.

4. Offline Discovery

The app constantly scans for other users with the app installed. When found, they are added to your local network list.

5. Local Broadcast

In emergencies, you can broadcast a message to everyone nearby, useful for SOS alerts.

6. Encryption

All private messages are end-to-end encrypted. Intermediate "hop" devices cannot read the content of the messages they relay.
