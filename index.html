Offline Mesh Communicator

Stay connected when the internet goes dark. A decentralized, Bluetooth-based communication network for emergencies.

Download APK (Demo) View GitHub

The Problem

Internet Shutdowns

During riots or government orders, internet access is often cut off, leaving citizens isolated.

Natural Disasters

Floods and earthquakes can destroy cell towers, making standard communication apps useless.

Communication Blackout

People cannot contact families or emergency services when they need it most.

The Solution

We create a local mesh network using Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE). Messages hop from device to device until they reach the destination, no internet required.

Bluetooth Mesh

Uses standard phone hardware to create a network.

Encrypted

End-to-end encryption ensures your messages stay private.

Offline Maps

Share last known locations even without GPS data.
