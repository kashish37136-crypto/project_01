Key Features

Everything you need to stay safe and connected.

Nearby Devices Scanning

Real-time radar UI showing distance to other devices in your vicinity.

Device List

See a list of active peers with signal strength indicators and estimated distance.

Offline Chat

Text messaging interface that works exactly like your favorite chat apps, but without internet.

Location Sharing

Share your last known location. Includes a compass-style direction indicator to find peers.

Settings & Permissions

Granular control over Bluetooth, Location access, and visibility settings.

Battery Optimization

Smart scanning intervals to ensure the app runs for days in emergency situations.
